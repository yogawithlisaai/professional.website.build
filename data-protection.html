Data Protection Policy

Effective Date: May 18, 2026

At Yoga with Lisa by Lisa Eshun-Wilson & Associates, LLC, we believe your personal information belongs to you. This page explains your rights regarding the data we collect, how to exercise those rights, and additional tools available to help you manage your privacy online.

For a full description of how we collect, use, and share your personal information, please review our Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have some or all of the following rights regarding your personal information:

Right to Know

You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you, including the categories of data collected, the purposes for which it is used, and any third parties with whom it is shared.

Right to Delete

You may request that we delete personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions (such as legal obligations or active service agreements).

Right to Correct

You may request that we correct inaccurate personal information we hold about you.

Right to Opt Out

You may request that we stop sharing your personal information with third parties for advertising or analytics purposes. Note that Yoga with Lisa does not sell your personal information for monetary consideration. However, certain analytics or third-party integrations may be considered "sharing" under applicable law.

Right to Data Portability

You may request a copy of your personal information in a portable, commonly used format.

Right to Non-Discrimination

We will never discriminate against you for exercising any of your privacy rights. Exercising these rights will not affect your access to our classes, programs, or services.

How to Submit a Request

To exercise any of the rights above, please contact us at:

Email: contact@yogawithlisa.ai
Subject line: "Data Protection Request"

Please include your full name and the email address associated with your account so we can verify your identity and locate your information. We will respond within 45 days consistent with applicable law. If we need more time, we will notify you in writing.

You may also update or delete your account information directly through your account settings on the Site.

California Residents

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) provide you with additional rights, including the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

To submit a CCPA/CPRA request, email contact@yogawithlisa.ai with the subject line "CCPA Data Protection Request." For more information, see the California Privacy Notice section of our Privacy Policy.

UK and EEA Residents

If you are located in the United Kingdom or European Economic Area, you have rights under the UK GDPR and EU GDPR, including the right to access, correct, erase, restrict, or object to processing of your personal data, and the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@yogawithlisa.ai.

Managing Cookies and Tracking

In addition to submitting a formal request, you can manage how your data is collected online using the following tools:

Browser Cookie Settings

Most browsers allow you to control or disable cookies through their settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features of the Site. Common browser settings can be found in the Options or Preferences menu of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

Advertising Opt-Out Tools

You can opt out of interest-based advertising through the following industry programs: the National Advertising Initiative (optout.networkadvertising.org) and the Digital Advertising Alliance (youradchoices.com).

Google Analytics

Our Site may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the Site. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using Google's browser add-on at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Privacy-Focused Browsers and Extensions

You can limit third-party tracking by using privacy-focused browsers such as Brave, or browser extensions such as Privacy Badger, Ghostery, or uBlock Origin.

Mobile Device Settings

Your mobile device may allow you to limit ad tracking through its privacy or advertising settings. For Apple devices, see support.apple.com. For Android devices, see google.com/policies/technologies/ads.

Social Media Connections

If you connect to our Site via a third-party platform such as Instagram or Facebook, you can manage what information is shared through that platform's own privacy settings.
