Before You Go

What you need to know.

Everything you need to prepare for your Costa Rica adventure.

✈️

Airport

Fly into Juan Santamaría International (SJO) in San José. A shared shuttle or private transfer brings you to the retreat center (~3–4 hrs). Transfer details sent after booking.

🛂

Entry Visa

US and most EU citizens don't need a visa for stays up to 90 days — just a valid passport (6+ months validity) and a return or onward ticket. Check requirements with your country's foreign affairs office.

💉

Vaccinations

No vaccinations are legally required. The CDC recommends staying current on routine vaccines plus Hepatitis A & B, and Typhoid. Speak with your doctor about dengue or malaria precautions.

🎒

What to Pack

Light yoga wear, a rain jacket (it's a rainforest!), reef-safe sunscreen, insect repellent, and personal medications. A full packing list is sent after registration.

Cancellation Policy

All sales are final.

We do not offer refunds for any reason, including illness, injury, travel disruptions, or personal circumstances. We strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance to protect your investment before booking.
