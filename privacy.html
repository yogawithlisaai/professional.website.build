Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy Effective Date: May 18, 2026 This Privacy Policy describes how Yoga with Lisa by Lisa Eshun-Wilson & Associates, LLC ("Yoga with Lisa," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you. This policy applies to information we collect when you use our website, app, online class library, membership portal, or otherwise interact with us as described below (collectively, the "Site"). We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by revising the date at the top of this policy, and in some cases may provide additional notice. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy whenever you interact with us to stay informed about our information practices. Acceptance of These Terms By using this Site on any computer, mobile phone, tablet, or other device, purchasing a class package or membership, or otherwise interacting with the Site, you signify your acceptance of this policy and any changes to it. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use our Site. Collection of Information We collect information from you directly, automatically when you visit the Site, and sometimes from third parties. Some of this information may be considered "Personal Information" under applicable laws. Information You Provide to Us. We collect information you provide directly, such as when you create an account, purchase a class package or membership, sign up for emails or notifications, book a private session or retreat, participate in a student wellness program, communicate with us via social media, request customer support, or otherwise contact us. The types of Personal Information we may collect include your name, email address, billing and shipping address, phone number, payment information, wellness goals, and any other information you choose to provide. Information We Collect Automatically. We automatically collect certain information when you access or use our Site, including: the type of browser you use, access times, pages viewed, your IP address, referring links, and information about your interactions with our content and classes. We may use cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies to collect this information and may combine it with other information we have about you to improve your experience and our services. Retention of Personal Information. We retain your Personal Information for as long as we consider it potentially useful in providing you with yoga and wellness services, or as needed to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. You may request deletion at any time by emailing lisa@yogawithlisa.ai. Use of Information We may use information about you to: provide and improve your online class experience; process transactions and send confirmations and receipts; respond to your questions and provide customer support; communicate with you about classes, schedules, memberships, retreats, wellness programs, and promotions; manage your account; personalize your experience; monitor and analyze usage trends; and carry out any other purpose for which the information was collected. Text and Email Communications: With your permission, we may send messages about class schedules, new content, wellness tips, and other updates. Your Information Choices and Controls You may correct, update, or delete your account information. You may change your subscription or notification preferences. You may request access to the Personal Information we hold about you, and ask that we amend or delete it. You can exercise these controls through the Site or by emailing lisa@yogawithlisa.ai. Sharing of Information We may share information about you: with third-party service providers who help us operate our Site and deliver services (such as payment processing, email delivery, and video hosting); as required by law or legal process; to protect the rights, property, and safety of Yoga with Lisa or others; in connection with a business transfer or acquisition; or with your consent. We do not sell your Personal Information to third parties for their own marketing purposes. Advertising and Analytics We may engage third parties to provide analytics services and, where applicable, to serve relevant content. These entities may use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your use of our Site. You can learn more about how to manage these preferences through your browser settings. Links to Third-Party Websites Our Site may contain links to third-party websites, including social media platforms and partner services. Our Privacy Policy does not apply to those websites, and we are not responsible for their privacy practices. Security We take reasonable measures to help protect your information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access. We use organizational, procedural, and technical safeguards consistent with the sensitivity of the data. However, no transmission of data over the internet is guaranteed to be completely secure. If you believe your interaction with us is no longer secure, please contact us immediately at lisa@yogawithlisa.ai. GDPR Notice for Residents of the United Kingdom or European Economic Area If you are resident in the UK or EEA, we will only process your Personal Data in compliance with applicable data protection laws, including the EU and UK General Data Protection Regulations. You have the right to request access to, correction of, erasure of, or restriction of processing of your Personal Data, as well as the right to data portability and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. To exercise these rights, please contact us at lisa@yogawithlisa.ai. Children We do not knowingly collect Personal Information from children under the age of 16. Our wellness programs for students are administered through institutional partnerships (such as schools), and individual student data is handled in accordance with applicable educational privacy laws. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 16, please contact us at lisa@yogawithlisa.ai. California Privacy Notice This notice applies to California residents and supplements our general Privacy Policy, as required by the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA). Your California Rights: You have the right to know what Personal Information we collect and how it is used; the right to request deletion of your Personal Information; the right to correct inaccurate information; the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your Personal Information; and the right to non-discrimination for exercising these rights. We do not sell your Personal Information for monetary consideration. However, certain sharing practices with analytics or advertising partners may be considered a "sale" or "share" under California law. To make a CCPA/CPRA request, email lisa@yogawithlisa.ai with the subject line "CCPA Rights Request." We will respond within 45 days consistent with applicable law. Other States' Rights If you are a resident of Colorado, Connecticut, Virginia, Texas, or other states with applicable privacy laws, you have similar rights to access, correct, delete, and opt out of the sale or sharing of your Personal Information. To make a request, contact lisa@yogawithlisa.ai. Contact Us If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us at: Yoga with Lisa by Lisa Eshun-Wilson & Associates, LLC Email: lisa@yogawithlisa.ai
