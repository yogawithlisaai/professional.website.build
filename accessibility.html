Web Accessibility Statement

General

Yoga with Lisa by Lisa Eshun-Wilson & Associates, LLC cares deeply about all of our students, members, and visitors, and is committed to ensuring that our website, yogawithlisa.ai (the "Site"), is accessible and welcoming to everyone — including persons with disabilities and users of assistive technology such as screen readers.

We are actively working to align our Site with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, which support conformance with the Americans with Disabilities Act of 1990 (ADA) and similar accessibility regulations in Canada, the United Kingdom, the European Union, and elsewhere. Our goal is to ensure that our yoga classes, wellness resources, and digital programs are available to all, regardless of ability.

Accessibility is especially meaningful to us given our mission of inclusivity and wellbeing. We believe that yoga and mindfulness should be available to every body, and that includes removing barriers to accessing our digital content.

Ongoing Efforts

We are continuously reviewing and improving our Site's accessibility. This includes testing with automated accessibility tools and working to remediate issues as they are discovered. Because assistive technology varies widely in its range and capabilities, we recognize that some users may encounter challenges, and we sincerely appreciate your patience and understanding as we continue to improve.

Disclaimer

While we strive to make the Site as accessible as possible, accessibility is an ongoing process. Some pages or features may not yet fully meet accessibility standards as we work through updates. We are committed to making consistent progress and welcome your feedback to help us prioritize improvements.

Contact Us

If you experience any difficulty accessing content on our Site, or if you have questions or concerns about the accessibility of any page or feature, please contact us:

Email: lisa@yogawithlisa.ai

When reaching out, please describe the specific page you were visiting and the nature of the issue you encountered. We will make all reasonable efforts to provide the information or content to you in an accessible format or to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

Thank you for visiting Yoga with Lisa. We are honored to be part of your wellness journey.
